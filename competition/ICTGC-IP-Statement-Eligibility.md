## II. Affidavit of Eligibility

**[Organization Name]** hereby confirms fulfillment of eligibility requirements:

### 1. Applicant Criteria

**(1) Startup Age Requirement**
- Companies must be established within **8 years** from registration date
- Does not apply to non-corporate entities, academic research teams, or individuals

**(2) China-Related Restrictions**
- **NOT eligible:** Startups/legal entities/academic institutions/individuals from China (including Hong Kong, Macau)
- **NOT eligible:** Startups established by Chinese entities in third areas

**(3) Funding Source Restrictions**
- Funding must NOT include funds from any individual, legal entity, or institution of China
- Includes third-area enterprises where China holds >30% shares or has controlling power

### 2. IP Ownership
- Participant must own IP rights of the product, technology, or solution
- OR have obtained legal licensing access

### 3. Submission Requirements
- Provide concept paper (initial review)
- Deliver presentation (secondary review)
- Submit **3-minute pitch video** with supporting materials

### 4. Previous Winner Restriction
- Previous award winners are not eligible
- Exception: Different project leader from previous competition

### 5. Violation Consequences
- Failure to meet requirements = forfeit place in competition
- Post-award violations = forfeit all awards and return monetary prizes
