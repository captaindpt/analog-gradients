# ICTGC Online Registration Guide

**Website:** [ictaiwanchallenge.org](https://ictaiwanchallenge.org)

---

## Step 1: Create Account

1. Go to the website and click **"Register Now"**
2. Click **"New User? Register"**
3. Fill in:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email

---

## Step 2: Receive Email Confirmation

You'll receive an email with:
- Account (Register Number): `ICTGC-XXXXXXXX`
- Password

---

## Step 3: Login

Use your Register Number and Password to login.

---

## Step 4: Complete Application Sections

### I. Basic Information for Organization
- Organization Name
- Website URL
- Contact Person details

### II. Basic Information for Product
- **Topic** (single choice):
  - Smart Data & AI (cyber-security, quantum computing, digital economy)
  - Smart Mobility (electric vehicles, autonomous driving, smart cities)
  - Smart Manufacturing (intelligent manufacturing, IC process, robots)
  - Smart Medtech (biometrics, smart monitoring)
  - Sustainability (sustainable manufacturing, energy-saving innovation)
- Name of Product or Solution

### III. Concept Paper
- Introduction to the Organization
- Background and Members' Experience
- (Text fields are mandatory; images optional, width 800px, <500KB)

### IV. IP Statement and Affidavit
1. Download the IP Statement & Affidavit of Eligibility document
2. Sign it
3. Upload in PDF format

**Important Note on Funding:**
> The source of funding must NOT include funds from a third-area enterprise where China (including Hong Kong, Macau) directly or indirectly holds more than 30% of shares or has controlling ability.

### V. Supplementary Documents
- Merge additional documents into one file
- Upload in PDF format (<5000 KB)

---

## Step 5: Submit

1. Review all sections
2. Click **"Submit"**
3. **Warning:** Once submitted, data cannot be modified!
4. Wait for notification of qualification results

---

## Tips

- **Save Draft** after finishing each section (system auto-logs out after 15 minutes idle)
- Fields marked with * are mandatory
- Contact: service@ictaiwanchallenge.org
